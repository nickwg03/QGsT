Landuse Layer
==================
  Source: U.S. Geological Survey Global Land Use/Land Cover dataset (1993)
  Spatial resolution/Scale: 1 km.
  Description:  Land use/land cover categories, using the U.S. Geological
    Survey Modified Level 2 legend.


Spatial Reference
==================
  Projection:  Albers
  Units: Meters
  Datum:  WGS84
  Parameters:
     False_Easting:	0
     False_Northing:	0
     Central_Meridian:	105.0
     Standard Parallel 1: 11.0
     Standard Parallel 2: 21.0
     Latitude_Of_Origin: 16.0
