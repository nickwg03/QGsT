Global Solar Layer
==================
  Source: National Renewable Energy Lab CSR dataset (2007)
  Spatial resolution/Scale: 30 km
  Description:  Global horizontal irradiance (GHI) averaged annually and
    monthly.


Spatial Reference
==================
  Projection:  Albers
  Units: Meters
  Datum:  WGS84
  Parameters:
     False_Easting:	0
     False_Northing:	0
     Central_Meridian:	105.0
     Standard Parallel 1: 11.0
     Standard Parallel 2: 21.0
     Latitude_Of_Origin: 16.0
